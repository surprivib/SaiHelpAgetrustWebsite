Our Services

SAI Helpage Trust works across multiple humanitarian sectors to uplift and empower the weaker and neglected sections of the society. Our core services ensure that basic needs, dignity, and opportunities are provided to all.

What We Do

Our Key Initiatives

Elderly Care & Old Age Support

We provide care, safety, and a homely environment for abandoned and senior citizens who need emotional, physical, and social support. Our goal is to ensure that no elder feels neglected or alone during their golden years.

Food Distribution & Hunger Relief

We conduct regular food distribution drives to serve fresh and nutritious meals to the underprivileged, homeless individuals, and struggling families, ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

Education Support for Children

We support children from marginalized communities by providing study materials, access to learning, and awareness programs, helping them build a bright and independent future.

Medical Assistance & Health Camps

Our medical outreach initiatives provide basic healthcare services, medicines, health awareness camps, and disease prevention guidance to economically weak families, promoting a healthier society.

Women Empowerment Support

We empower women through skills training, awareness workshops, and support initiatives to build independent and confident lives.

Disaster & Emergency Relief Aid

We actively help families affected by floods, fires, pandemics, and other crises with immediate relief and rehabilitation support.

Our organization remains committed to creating lasting social change with compassion, equality, and integrity at the core of every initiative.
