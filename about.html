About The Trust

SAI Helpage Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving humanity and improving the lives of those in need. Our mission is to provide dignity, support, and better living conditions for the weaker sections of society through compassionate and sustainable initiatives.

We focus on creating a secure and respectful living environment for senior citizens by providing shelter, care, and emotional support through our Old Age Care services. Our aim is to ensure that the elderly are never left abandoned or alone.

Hunger should never be a barrier to survival â€” therefore, we run regular food distribution programs offering nutritious meals to poor families, homeless individuals, and communities struggling with poverty and unemployment.

Education is the foundation of progress. We assist children from low-income families by providing educational support, learning materials, and motivation to ensure they have the opportunity to pursue a brighter future.

For those who cannot afford healthcare, we extend medical support including basic treatment, medicine assistance, and awareness programmes to promote a healthier society. Our goal is to eliminate suffering that arises due to lack of resources.

At SAI Helpage Trust, we believe in equality, compassion, and collective growth. Through our services, we strive to build a society where the elderly feel loved, children are educated, the poor are fed, and every individual receives medical care for a dignified life.

80G Certificate

Donations made to SAI Helpage Trust are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Donors can claim up to 50% tax exemption on their contributions.
