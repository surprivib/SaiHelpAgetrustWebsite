Who We Are

About SAI Helpage Trust

SAI Helpage Trust is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting underprivileged communities. We focus on elderly care, food distribution, education support, and healthcare for those who need it most.

With compassion at our core, we strive to build a society where every individual can live with dignity, equality, and hope.

Why Choose Us

Dedicated to Service

Hunger Relief & Seasonal Support

We serve meals to underprivileged communities and provide essential winter supplies such as blankets, ensuring dignity and warmth for those in need.

Elderly Care & Assistance

We extend care and support to senior citizens who are alone, neglected, or living in difficult situations, helping them live with respect and comfort.

Healthcare & Education Aid

We provide essential medicines and distribute educational materials including notebooks and stationery to children from low-income families, supporting their health and learning.
